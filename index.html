About Us

Think Health Consultants Limited is a regional leader in health systems research, epidemiology, and evidence-based policy consulting. With a foundation built on over two decades of combined experience in public health, health systems strengthening, epidemiological innovation, and academic training, we provide cutting-edge solutions to pressing health and development challenges.

We specialize in health policy analysis, systems design, implementation research, and strategic advisory services for governments, multilateral organizations, academic institutions, and NGOs across Africa and globally.

Our Vision

To be the premier African consultancy and research firm in health systems innovation and policy, catalysing equitable and sustainable health outcomes through transformative solutions.

Our Mission

To generate, synthesize, and translate multidisciplinary research into impactful health policies and practices through collaboration, innovation, and capacity development.
